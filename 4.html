Math skill in an engineer:

A Mini Project Competition for the pre-final year engineering students is planned with an intention to nurture the skills to express mathematical solutions through any of the following technologies. The projects are exhibited to nearby school and PU students through online which enhance the interest of mathematics in a technical way. Following are the technologies applied in the projects:


The concepts of Web applications can be merged with Mathematics by using the:

  • Boolean Logic which could be used to write the conditional statements of the business logic.

  • Statistical models can be used in Web applications which could be used for analytics of the data.
